<blockquote> <p>A block quotation (also known as a long quotation or extract) is a quotation in a written document, that is set off from the main text as a paragraph, or block of text.</p> <p>It is typically distinguished visually using indentation and a different typeface or smaller size quotation. It may or may not include a citation, usually placed at the bottom.</p> <cite><a href="#!">Said no one, ever.</a></cite> </blockquote>
